Admissions

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds and abilities. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic community and contribute to our school's culture.

To apply, please follow the steps below:

  1. Fill out the online application form
  2. Submit the application fee
  3. Provide transcripts and test scores
  4. Complete an interview with our admissions team

If you have any questions about the admissions process or requirements, please contact our admissions office at admissions@school.edu or (123) 456-7890.

Admissions FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about our admissions process.

What are the admission requirements?

To apply, you must have a high school diploma or equivalent, submit an application, and meet our minimum GPA and test score requirements.

How do I apply?

You can apply online or in person. Visit our admissions page for more information and to start your application.

When is the application deadline?

The application deadline for our upcoming semester is August 1st. Please check our website for updates and changes.
